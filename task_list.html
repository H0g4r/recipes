Apple pie

Ingredients:

Steps:

1

Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit/220 degrees Celsius.

2

In a small bowl, mix sugars, cornstarch, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt.

3

Place apples in a large bowl and sprinkle sugar mixture and cinnamon over apples.

4

Toss gently and let stand for ten minutes or until a little juice forms.

5

Prepare pie crusts in a nine-inch pie plate. When trimming edge, leave 1⁄2 inch (1.3 cm) overhang.

6

Place pile of apple-sugar mixture into bottom pastry/crust and drop in clumps of butter or margarine.

7

Moisten overhanging edge of bottom pastry before placing top pastry and trimming overhang to one inch

8

With a sharp paring knife, cut decorative slits on the top of the pastry to allow steam to escape.

9

Place pie in preheated oven( 220 degrees Celsius )and bake for 40 minutes.

10

Once the pie is done, take it out of the oven and let cool for about 15 minutes before serving.

11

Enjoy this delicious dessert!

12

Finished.

Cheesecake

Ingredients:

Crust:

Filling:

Making the Crust:

1

Choose your pan wisely. Cheesecakes are notoriously crumbly, so choosing the right pan will ensure that your cheesecake comes out smoothly.

2

Cover the pan with aluminum foil. For the best cheesecake you’ve ever eaten, you’ll need to bake it surrounded by boiling water.

3

Place a rack in the lower third of the oven. Once you have done this, preheat the oven to 350 °F (177 °C)(176c).

4

Pour the graham cracker dust into a large bowl.Use a spatula to stir in the salt and sugar,making sure that all of the ingredients are thoroughly mixed.

5

Pour the crust mixture into the pan. Save about ¼ cup of the mixture to use later if need be.

6

Place the pan in the oven. The crust will need to harden up a bit--putting it in the oven for 10 minutes should achieve the desired texture.

Making the Filling:

1

Cut your cream cheese into large chunks. One by one, cut away a chunk of the cream cheese and place it into the bowl of your electric mixture.

2

Add the sugar to the cream cheese. Pour your sugar into the bowl and mix the two ingredients together for four minutes.

3

Crack the eggs into the bowl on at a time. When you add one egg, turn the mixer on and beat it for one minute. Repeat this process with the three remaining eggs.

4

Pour the filling into the pan on top of the crust. Make sure that you scoop out all of the filling while also making sure that it doesn’t overflow the pan.

Baking the Cheesecake:

1

Place the springboard pan into a high-sided roasting pan. Bring two quarts of water to a boil.

2

Place the pan inside a pan on the lower rack of the oven. Set your timer for one and a half hours and let your delicious cheesecake bake.

3

Turn off the heat. Open the door of the oven so that it is about an inch open. Let the cheesecake cool in the oven.

4

Cover the top of your cheesecake with foil and place it in the fridge.You will want to leave it in the fridge four hours.

5

Remove your cheesecake from the pan. Serve and enjoy!
